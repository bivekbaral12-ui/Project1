Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a special license?
Yes, you need a valid motorcycle license (Category A) from your home country or an International Driving Permit.
Is insurance included in the price?
Basic third-party insurance is included. Comprehensive coverage is available for an additional Rs. 500 per day.
Do I need to leave a deposit?
Yes, we require a refundable security deposit of Rs. 10,000 and a physical copy of your passport or citizenship card.
